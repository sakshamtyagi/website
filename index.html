This pioneering course provides comprehensive knowledge of music distribution through a structured and sequential series of topics and chapters. "The Distribution Blueprint" empowers independent artists and aspiring distributors with in-depth insights and practical strategies.


WHAT THIS COURSE OFFERS:


1. Curriculum Development -

Designed to cover all aspects of music distribution, from the basics to advanced techniques.

2. Content Creation -

Engaging and informative high-quality video lectures that make complex concepts easy to understand.

3. Authorship -

A detailed chapter-wise book accompanying the video lectures, serving as a valuable reference guide.

4. Community Building -

Engagement with a growing community of YouTube subscribers, offering continuous support and updates on the latest industry trends.

5. Empowerment -

Providing tools, resources, and knowledge that enable independent artists to take control of their music careers and achieve greater success.
